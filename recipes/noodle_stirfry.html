Noodle Stir Fry

Description

This is another experiment in resourcefulness. Take whatever veggies you have, saute them, add sauce, make noodles, mix and enjoy.

Ingredients

This is by no means an exhaustive list of ingredients. If you don't have everything listed here, or want to add things that aren't on the list, feel free. Consider these more like guidelines than hard and fast instructions.
  • yellow or white onion
  • bell pepper
  • mushrooms
  • green onions
  • soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • sesame oil
  • gochujang
  • honey

Equipment

  • Cutting Board
  • Knife
  • Saute Pan (Cast iron if you can)
  • Sauce pan
  • Pot
  • Strainer

Directions

  1. in saucepan mix two parts soy sauce to .5 parts each rice vinegar and sesame oil
  2. add a dash of honey and gochugang (a little goes a long way)
  3. wash and cut green onions as thinly as you can
  4. add to soy sauce mixture, set over medium low heat
  5. cut up all veggies, throw in saute pan over medium high heat
  6. stir regulary
  7. boil water for noodles, add noodles, strain when done.
  8. add noodles to veggies, pour sauce mixture on top and mix all together
  9. plate and serve
